Meet Dr. Mitch

Patients know him as Dr. Mitch—Mitchell Chen, MD. He treats athletes, weekend warriors, and anyone sidelined by joint pain or injury. His approach pairs clear communication with surgical precision when surgery is truly the best option, and conservative care when it isn’t.

Whether you need a second opinion on a knee replacement, a torn rotator cuff evaluated, or a fracture set and followed to healing, you’ll get a straightforward plan and respectful, unrushed visits.

Conditions & treatments

Comprehensive orthopedic care for adults and teens, with emphasis on returning you to work and play safely.

Hip & knee replacement

Primary and revision joint replacement, anterior and traditional approaches, rapid-recovery pathways, and robotic-assisted options when appropriate.

Sports medicine

ACL, meniscus, and cartilage injuries; shoulder instability; tennis elbow; PRP and injection therapies as clinically indicated.

Shoulder & elbow

Rotator cuff repair, labral tears, frozen shoulder, distal biceps and common tendon problems—surgical and non-surgical pathways.

Fracture care

Acute fracture management, casting and splinting, and coordinated follow-up with physical therapy partners.

Knee & leg

Patellofemoral pain, osteoarthritis bracing and injections, hamstring and quadriceps strains, and overuse injuries.

Work & everyday injuries

Carpal tunnel evaluation, hand and wrist sprains, and ergonomic-related upper extremity complaints.
